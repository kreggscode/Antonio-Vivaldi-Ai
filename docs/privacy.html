Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 29, 2025

Welcome to Antonio Vivaldi AI. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

The app stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Favorites: Quotes and content you mark as favorites
  • Preferences: Your app settings including dark mode preference and notification settings
  • Chat History: Your conversations with the AI (stored locally only)

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use the AI chat feature, the following information is sent to our AI service provider (Pollinations AI):

  • Chat Messages: Your questions and the AI's responses for context
  • Conversation Context: Recent message history to maintain conversation flow

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect or receive:

  • To provide and maintain the app's functionality
  • To enable AI chat conversations about Antonio Vivaldi and Baroque music
  • To save your preferences and favorites locally on your device
  • To send you daily notifications if you enable this feature
  • To improve app performance and user experience

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

All your personal data (favorites, preferences, chat history) is stored locally on your device using:

  • Room Database: For favorites and quotes
  • DataStore: For app preferences and settings

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly uninstall the app, in which case it is permanently deleted.

3.2 Third-Party Services

We use Pollinations AI for chat functionality. When you use the chat feature:

  • Your messages are sent to Pollinations AI servers for processing
  • Pollinations AI does not store your conversations permanently
  • No personal identifying information is sent with your messages
  • The service is used solely to generate AI responses

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. Your data is:

  • Not collected by us on any server
  • Not shared with advertisers or marketing companies
  • Not used for tracking or analytics
  • Only processed by Pollinations AI when you use the chat feature

5. Your Privacy Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: All your data is stored locally and accessible to you at any time
  • Deletion: You can clear chat history, remove favorites, or uninstall the app to delete all data
  • Control: You control what you save as favorites and what preferences you set
  • Opt-Out: You can disable notifications at any time in the app settings

6. Children's Privacy

Antonio Vivaldi AI is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. All data is stored locally on the device, and we do not maintain any servers that collect user information.

7. Internet Connection

The app requires an internet connection only for:

  • AI chat functionality (communicating with Pollinations AI)
  • Opening external links (Google Play Store, developer website)

All other features work completely offline.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy
  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and on our website

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.

9. Third-Party Links

The app may contain links to third-party websites (Google Play Store, developer website). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party sites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies.

10. Data Retention

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Data persists until you manually delete it or uninstall the app
  • Chat history can be cleared at any time using the "Clear Chat" button
  • Favorites can be removed individually or cleared entirely
  • Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all local data
